# doc_id: HR-02
# Title: Equipment Allocation Regulations
# Keywords: Laptop, Monitor, Equipment, Return, Inventory

## Basic Allocation
1. **Laptop:** One unit allocated upon joining. Specifications vary by job level.
2. **Monitor:** One additional unit can be allocated upon request.
3. **Return:** All allocated equipment must be returned upon resignation.

## In Case of Stock Shortage
1. Check inventory (equipment_inventory); if insufficient, request a purchase.
2. Temporarily allocate rental equipment until the purchase is complete.
