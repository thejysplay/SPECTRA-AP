# Title: Permission Management Policy
# Keywords: permission, access, administrator, general, least privilege

## Permission Granting Principles
1. **Least Privilege:** Grant only the minimum permissions necessary for the job.
2. **Administrator Privileges:** Requires a team leader position or higher + approval from the HR team leader.
3. **Temporary Permissions:** Maximum of 30 days. Automatically revoked upon expiration.

## Prohibitions
1. Prohibit granting administrator privileges to general employees.
2. Prohibit reactivating permissions for accounts of resigned employees.
