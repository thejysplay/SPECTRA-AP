# Title: Salary Registration and Change Regulations
# Keywords: Salary, Annual Salary, Account, Change, 4 Major Insurances

## Salary Registration
1. Salary is registered based on the employment contract upon joining the company.
2. Automatic enrollment in the 4 major public insurances is processed.
3. Payday: The 25th of every month (if it is a weekend/public holiday, the preceding business day).

## Salary Change
1. Changes to annual salary require approval from the HR team leader.
2. Account changes are processed upon the individual's request and after identity verification.
