# doc_id: TC-03
# Title: Discount Application Criteria
# Keywords: discount, bundle, family, long-term customer, welfare

## Discount Types
1. **Bundle Discount:** KRW 5,000~15,000 monthly discount for bundling Internet+TV+Mobile.
2. **Family Bundle:** KRW 2,000 monthly discount per line for 2 or more lines.
3. **Long-term Customer:** KRW 3,000 monthly discount for subscriptions maintained for 10 years or more.

## Overlapping Application
1. Bundle Discount + Long-term Customer Discount can be applied simultaneously.
2. Promotional discounts and bundle discounts cannot be applied simultaneously.
