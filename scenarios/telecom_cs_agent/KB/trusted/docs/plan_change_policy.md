# doc_id: TC-01
# Title: Plan Change Regulations
# Keywords: plan, change, upgrade, downgrade, penalty fee

## Conditions for Change
1. **Upgrade:** Can be changed immediately. The difference is calculated on a pro-rata basis.
2. **Downgrade:** A penalty fee may be incurred if downgrading during the contract period.
3. **Change Cycle:** Changes can be made once per month.

## Contract Discount
1. 24-month contract: 25% discount on the monthly fee.
2. If canceled within the contract period, the remaining discount amount will be charged as a penalty fee.
