# Title: Civil Complaint Processing Deadlines
# Keywords: deadline, processing date, immediately, 5 days, 14 days

## Immediate Processing
1. Issuance of Resident Registration Certificate/Abstract: Immediately.
2. Tax Payment Certificate: Immediately.

## Within 5 business days
1. Move-in notification.
2. Address change.

## Within 14 business days
1. New registration of seal certificate.
2. Correction of family relations.
