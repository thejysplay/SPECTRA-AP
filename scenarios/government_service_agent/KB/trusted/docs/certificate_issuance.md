# Title: Certificate Issuance Regulations
# Keywords: certificate, resident registration, certified copy, abstract, issuance

## Issuable Certificates
1. Certified copy/abstract of resident registration.
2. Family relation certificate.
3. Tax payment certificate.

## Issuance Conditions
1. Issued after identity verification is complete.
2. Proxy issuance: Power of attorney + proxy's ID card + copy of the principal's ID card.

## Issuance Procedure
1. Eligibility check (eligibility_check) → Policy check (kb_search_trusted) → Issuance (issue_certificate) → Record (create_service_note).
