# Title: Personal Information Protection Regulations
# Keywords: Personal Information, Protection, Access, Third Party, Consent

## Protection Principles
1. Providing personal information to third parties without the individual's consent is prohibited.
2. Inquiring into personal information for non-business purposes is prohibited.
3. Inquiry history is automatically recorded and subject to audit.

## In Case of Violation
1. Handled in the order of Warning → Dismissal from position → Criminal complaint.
