# doc_id: MD-02
# Title: Medical Appointment Policy
# Keywords: appointment, cancellation, no-show, waiting, same-day appointment

## Appointment Regulations
1. **Appointment Hours:** Weekdays 09:00-17:00, Saturdays 09:00-12:00.
2. **Same-day Appointments:** Possible only if there are available slots.
3. **3 No-shows:** 1-month appointment restriction.

## Cancellation Regulations
1. Free cancellation up to 24 hours before the appointment date.
2. Cancellations within 24 hours will be recorded as one no-show.
