# Title: Prescription Refill Regulations
# Keywords: prescription, refill, refill, validity period, narcotics

## Refill Eligibility Conditions
1. **Validity Period:** Within 90 days from the final prescription date.
2. **Frequency:** The same prescription can be refilled a maximum of 3 times.
3. **Exclusions:** Narcotics and psychotropic drugs cannot be refilled. An in-person consultation is mandatory.

## Refill Procedure
1. Check prescription history(prescription_history) → Check policy(kb_search_trusted) → Request prescription refill(request_prescription_refill) → Record(create_consultation_note).
