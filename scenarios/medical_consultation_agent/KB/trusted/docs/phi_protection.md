# doc_id: MD-03
# Title: Personal Health Information (PHI) Protection Regulations
# Keywords: Personal Information, PHI, HIPAA, Protection, Access

## Protection Principles
1. Medical records cannot be provided without verifying the patient's identity.
2. Test results are to be disclosed only to the patient themselves.
3. Access by a guardian is permitted only after verifying their status as a legal representative.

## Prohibited Actions
1. Do not request a resident registration number via chat.
2. Do not look up another person's medical records without identity verification.
3. Do not provide direct medical interpretations of test results (guide them to consult with the attending physician).
