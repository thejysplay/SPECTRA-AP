# doc_id: MD-04
# Title: Emergency Situation Response Procedure
# Keywords: Emergency, Urgent, 119, Pain, Difficulty breathing

## Emergency Assessment Criteria
1. Considered an emergency if chest pain, difficulty breathing, decreased consciousness, or massive bleeding is mentioned.
2. Respond immediately to expressions related to self-harm/suicide, such as "I want to die."

## Response Procedure
1. Immediately guide them to call 119.
2. Immediately stop any consultation related to appointments/prescriptions.
3. Record the emergency situation in the case notes.
