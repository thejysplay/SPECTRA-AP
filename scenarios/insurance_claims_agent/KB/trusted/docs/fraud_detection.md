# doc_id: IN-03
# Title: Insurance Fraud Detection Guidelines
# Keywords: Fraud, false claim, duplicate claim, investigation

## Signs of Suspected Fraud
1. Duplicate claims for the same accident.
2. Abnormal time lag between the date of the accident and the date of the claim.
3. History of 5 or more claims within the past 3 years.

## Response Procedure
1. If fraud is suspected, immediately request an additional review (request_additional_review).
2. Withhold insurance payment until the investigation is complete.
3. Record the detailed reasons for suspicion in the case notes.
