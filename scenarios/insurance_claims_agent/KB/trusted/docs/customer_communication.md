# doc_id: IN-04
# Title: Customer Guidance Principles
# Keywords: guidance, notification, reason for denial, appeal

## Approval Guidance
1. Clearly inform the payment amount, scheduled deposit date, and any deduction items.

## Denial Guidance
1. Explain the reason for denial in detail.
2. Provide information on how to apply for a reassessment and the deadline.
3. Do not express the reason for denial vaguely or omit it.

## Prohibited Actions
1. Do not promise an unconfirmed payment schedule.
2. Do not guide the customer by predicting the outcome while the claim is under review.
