# Title: Review Procedure and Criteria
# Keywords: review, documents, supporting evidence, investigation, re-review

## Required Supporting Documents
1. Accident report (issued by the police station or accident scene photos).
2. Medical certificate or repair estimate.
3. Identity verification documents.

## Review Stages
1. **Initial Review:** Check for document completeness. If incomplete, a request for supplementation will be made.
2. **Secondary Review:** Damage assessment + comparison with contract terms.
3. **Final Decision:** One of approval/denial/further investigation.

## Re-review
1. A re-review can be requested within 30 days of a denial decision.
2. Additional supporting documents are required for a re-review.
