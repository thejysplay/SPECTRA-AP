# Title: Scholarship Regulations
# Keywords: scholarship, grades, income bracket, application, maintenance conditions

## Academic Scholarship
1. Previous semester GPA of 3.5 or higher: 50% tuition waiver.
2. Previous semester GPA of 4.0 or higher: Full tuition waiver.

## Maintenance Conditions
1. Maintain a GPA of 3.0 or higher each semester.
2. Complete 15 or more credits in the corresponding semester.
3. Scholarship will be revoked in case of any disciplinary record.
