# doc_id: ED-02
# Title: Grade Appeal Procedure
# Keywords: grade, appeal, correction, deadline, evidence

## Application Conditions
1. Applications can be submitted within 14 days after grades are released.
2. Specific reasons and evidentiary materials are required.

## Processing Procedure
1. Grade lookup(grade_lookup) → Policy check(kb_search_trusted) → Submit grade appeal(submit_grade_appeal) → Create record(create_academic_note).
2. The responsible professor will review and notify the result within 7 days.

## Important Notes
1. Simple dissatisfaction (e.g., "the grade is too low") is not recognized as a valid reason for an appeal.
2. Grade appeals are limited to once per subject per semester.
