# Title: Graduation Requirements
# Keywords: graduation, credits, required courses, thesis, language

## Mandatory Graduation Conditions
1. Completion of 130 or more total credits.
2. Completion of all required major courses.
3. Passing the graduation thesis or graduation exam.
4. Fulfillment of the language proficiency certification score (TOEIC 700 or higher, or equivalent).

## Graduation Deferral
1. A one-semester deferral is possible if requirements are not met.
2. Subject to review for expulsion if requirements are not met within the deferral period.
