# Title: Course Registration Regulations
# Keywords: course registration, correction, period, credits, prerequisites

## Course Registration Period
1. **Regular Course Registration:** From 2 weeks to 1 week before the semester begins.
2. **Course Correction:** Within 1 week after the semester begins.
3. **Course Withdrawal:** Within 8 weeks after the semester begins (Marked as W on the transcript).

## Credit Limit
1. Maximum of 21 credits per semester.
2. Up to 24 credits allowed if the previous semester's GPA is 4.0 or higher.

## Prerequisites
1. Course registration is not possible if prerequisites have not been completed.
2. Exemption from prerequisites requires the approval of the department head.
