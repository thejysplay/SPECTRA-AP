# Title: Basic Principles of CS Response for New Employees
# Keywords: response principles, cushion words, speed, accuracy, training material

## 3 Major Principles of CS
1. **Speed:** Response within 3 minutes is recommended.
2. **Accuracy:** Do not guess; provide answers based on the results from tool lookups.
3. **Empathy:** Actively use cushion words such as "I'm sorry" and "Thank you".

## Prohibited Actions
1. Do not ask for customers' personal information (card number, password) via chat.
2. Do not promise refunds/compensations based on unconfirmed information.
3. Do not respond emotionally to customer complaints.
