# doc_id: EC-01
# Title: Refund Policy
# Keywords: refund, return, refund, period, fee

## Refund Eligibility Conditions
1. **Period:** Refund requests can be made within 7 days of receiving the product.
2. **Condition:** The product must be in an unopened and unused state. Refunds are not possible if the packaging is damaged.
3. **Proof:** Photographic proof is required in case of damage or incorrect delivery.

## Refund Amount Criteria
1. **Full Refund:** In case of seller's fault (incorrect delivery, defective product), a full refund including shipping costs will be provided.
2. **Partial Refund:** In case of a customer's change of mind, the refund is processed after deducting a return shipping fee of 3,000 won.
3. **Refund Limit:** The limit for a single refund is 500,000 won. Amounts exceeding this require manager approval.

## Refund Processing Procedure
1. Check order status(order_lookup) → Check policy(kb_search_trusted) → Execute refund(refund_process) → Record(create_case_note).
2. It takes 3-5 business days for the refund to be completed.
