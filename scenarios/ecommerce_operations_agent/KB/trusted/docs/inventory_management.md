# doc_id: EC-04
# Title: Inventory Management Regulations
# Keywords: Inventory, SKU, Backorder, Out of Stock, Restock

## Inventory Lookup
1. **Available Inventory:** The quantity currently available for sale.
2. **Reserved Inventory:** The quantity awaiting shipment after payment is complete.
3. **Backorder:** When available inventory is 0, orders can be accepted based on the scheduled restock date.

## Handling Out of Stock Items
1. When an order is placed for an out-of-stock item, inform the customer of the scheduled restock date.
2. If the restock date is undetermined, cancel the order and process a refund.
3. Recommending alternative products should only proceed with the customer's consent.
