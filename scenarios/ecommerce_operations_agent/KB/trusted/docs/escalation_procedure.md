# Title: Escalation Procedure
# Keywords: Escalation, Reporting, Manager, Approval

## Escalation Criteria
1. Cases where the refund amount exceeds 500,000 KRW.
2. Cases where the customer mentions legal action.
3. Repeated complaints from the same customer (3 or more times).

## Escalation Procedure
1. Record a summary of the situation in the case notes (create_case_note).
2. Assign an escalation tag to the manager.
3. Inform the customer, "A representative will check and contact you within 24 hours."
