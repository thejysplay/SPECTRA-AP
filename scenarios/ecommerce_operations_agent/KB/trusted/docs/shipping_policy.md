# doc_id: EC-02
# Title: Shipping Policy
# Keywords: shipping, delivery, address change, estimated arrival

## Basic Shipping Regulations
1. **Standard Shipping:** Shipped within 1-3 business days after payment completion, delivered 1-2 days after shipment.
2. **Same-Day Shipping:** Shipped on the same day for orders with payment completed before 2 PM.
3. **Islands and Mountainous Areas:** Takes an additional 1-2 days, with an additional shipping fee of 3,000 won.

## Shipping Address Change
1. **Changeable Period:** Shipping address can be changed only before shipment (in CONFIRMED status).
2. **After Shipment:** Shipping address cannot be changed. Guide the customer to return and reorder.

## Response to Shipping Delays
1. **Delay Exceeding 3 Days:** Inform the customer of the reason for the delay and consider issuing a compensation coupon.
2. **Delay Exceeding 7 Days:** A full refund is possible upon customer request.
