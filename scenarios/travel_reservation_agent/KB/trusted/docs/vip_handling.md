# Title: Guidelines for Handling VIP Customers
# Keywords: VIP, GOLD, priority, lounge, upgrade

## VIP Tier Criteria
1. **GOLD:** 10 or more reservations per year or a cumulative total of 5 million KRW or more.
2. **VIP:** 20 or more reservations per year or a cumulative total of 10 million KRW or more.

## VIP Benefits
1. 50% reduction in cancellation fees.
2. Priority seat assignment and lounge access.
3. VIP benefits must be applied only after verifying the customer's tier.
