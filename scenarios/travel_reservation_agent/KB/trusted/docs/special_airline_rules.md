# Title: Special Regulations by Airline
# Keywords: Airline, LCC, Baggage, Seat Assignment, Regulations

## Korean Air (KE) / Asiana Airlines (OZ)
1. 1 free checked baggage (23kg) included.
2. Free seat assignment.

## Low-Cost Carriers (LJ, 7C, TW)
1. Baggage must be purchased separately (15,000 KRW for 15kg).
2. Paid seat assignment (3,000~8,000 KRW).
3. Cancellation fee: 50% before 7 days of departure, 100% within 7 days.
