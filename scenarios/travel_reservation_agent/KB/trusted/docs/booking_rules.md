# Title: Reservation Creation Regulations
# Keywords: reservation, payment, seat, room, confirmation

## Reservation Confirmation Conditions
1. **Flights:** Automatically confirmed upon payment completion. Payment deadline is 24 hours.
2. **Hotels:** Automatically confirmed upon payment completion. The free cancellation deadline varies by hotel.
3. **Duplicate reservations:** In case of duplicate reservations for the same date and same route, only one will be maintained after verification.

## Reservation Changes
1. Date/time can be changed once up to 48 hours before departure.
2. Passenger name changes are not permitted.
3. Change fee: KRW 30,000 for flights, free for hotels.
