# Title: Warehouse Management Regulations
# Keywords: warehouse, inventory, receiving, shipping, storage

## Receiving and Shipping Regulations
1. Quantity inspection is mandatory upon receiving.
2. First-In, First-Out (FIFO) principle for shipping.
3. Long-term storage fees are charged for storage periods exceeding 90 days.

## Inventory Management
1. Daily physical inventory count (9 AM).
2. In case of inventory discrepancy, immediately record it in the operations note.
